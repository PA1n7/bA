Behavioural addiction

An addictive behavior is the compulsive use of substances and activities ( like sex and gambling ), without the problem of the negative consequences that it can have, these can be physical and/or mentally, but people used it to “complete their need, or to fight pain temporally”.

Main Causes

Mainly, the causes of this types of problems comes from exposure to addictive substances, social pressure, and a being easily manipulated, and if you already have use some of this substances, you may develop an extremely addictive need to use them.
